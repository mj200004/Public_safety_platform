# 🛡️ Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform

An enterprise-grade, proactive AI intelligence layer designed to neutralize cross-border digital arrest syndicates and high-denomination counterfeit currency rings before mass victimization occurs.

## 🚀 The Core Problem
In 2024, digital arrest scams defrauded citizens of over **₹1,776 crore**. Meanwhile, manual point-of-contact banking checks frequently fail to detect sophisticated ₹500 counterfeits. Law enforcement currently operates on *reactive* data silos.

## 🧠 Our Solution
We transition law enforcement from reactive reporting to **predictive intervention** by fusing two core AI engines:
1. **Forensic Computer Vision:** Instant structural and microprint analysis using OpenCV to verify physical currency integrity.
2. **Topological Graph Intelligence:** Multi-dimensional telemetry mapping using NetworkX to instantly identify scammer hubs and financial mule accounts.
